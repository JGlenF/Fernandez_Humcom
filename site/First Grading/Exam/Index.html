II. Telehealth and telemedicine

by Anis Uzzaman

Source: https://www.inc.com/anis-uzzaman/top-business-technology-trends-in-2021
Institutions, especially in health care, are working to lower the exposure of Covid-19 to patients and workers. Many private and public practices have started implementing more telehealth offerings such as doctor-patient video chats, A.I. avatar-based diagnostics, and no-contact-based medication delivery
Telehealth visits have surged by 50 percent compared with pre-pandemic levels. IHS Technology predicted that 70 millionAmericans would use telehealth by 2020. Since then, ForresterResearch predicted the number of U.S. virtual care visits willreach almost a billion early in 2021.
